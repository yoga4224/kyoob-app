Xaxis Launches First Mobile Audio Network

Mobile specialist Xaxis, has launched a mobile audio network, Big Audio.

Created through a strategic partnership with Triton Digital & Southern Cross Austereo's a2x Digital Audio Exchange, Big Audio incorporates audiences across multiple leading music streaming services and radio broadcasters listening on mobile apps and sites.

17JUL

Xaxis Appoints Co-Founder As New CEO

Co-founder, Graham Christie has been appointed as Group CEO, one of a number of changes taking place following a successful and transformative FY15 for Xaxis.

The company is making significant investments in technologies and people, bigger premises in Sydney and Singapore, regional expansion, and a restructure all focused to sustain the companies accelerated growth over the next three years.

20MAR

Xaxis Launches Australia Largest Premium Mobile Video Network

Independent mobile specialist, Xaxis, has launched Big Video, a premium-only mobile video network with private deals across seven leading Australian publishers, both mobile and tablet, including APN Australian Regional Media, and Southern Cross Austereo.

The new Big Video network includes over 150 sites and apps with four million monthly unique browsers (UB) and 100 million monthly impressions, across a compelling variety of formats.

Wings Corp Appoints Xaxis As Exclusive Mobile Specialist Partner

Xaxis, Indonesia's leading independent mobile specialist, has been appointed the exclusive Mobile Agency partner for Wings Corp, Indonesia's leading manufacturer & distributor of household products.
